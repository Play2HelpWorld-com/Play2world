Token design

PLAY2WORLD is minted solely by verified game. Therefore, a stable number of users increases minting “input”. An increase in users increases the input. A change in user habits – all users playing more because of intention and rewards – makes the input rise exponentially.
Each PLAY2WORLD will require more points to mint than the last. To achieve our vision of making the world more active, PLAY2WORLD will be harder to mint with time. Not only does this incentivize users to get moving now – as their Play generates more PLAY2WORLD today than tomorrow – it preserves the utility of PLAY2WORLD as the model represents an ever increasing marginal cost of production.

The open economy of the game is enabled by PLAY2WORLD. It is a new market, a new economy, in which anyone, anywhere, can be rewarded in increasingly attractive ways, for any form of activity.
Play Validators are required to verify the validity of Play and update the blockchain accordingly. Consistent and accurate Play validation is fundamental to ensuring the integrity of the underlying asset

The current process at Play2Worldcoin – and the process applied to the launch claim – is such that: for every 1,000 verified points, 1 Play2World coin is awarded. The validator receives the raw data from the recording device, and implements an algorithm to detect fraudulent behavior, and ensure only true Play is rewarded. Play2World Ltd is the only Play Validator as of launch.
Going forward, we intend to decentralize the validation process to enable third parties, such as activity trackers, playing games, and app developers to operate as Play Validators. These tracking services will have to stake PLAY2WORLD to operate as Play Validators. In return, for their contribution, they will receive a % fee for all transactions they process. By decentralization, we enable new markets for different types of activity, and expand the breadth of the open economy of Play.

Play Validators receive 5% of the PLAY2WORLD they contribute to the minting of. This is the same fee that Play2World Ltd has earned for its verification of points via the Play2World coin App.

Power to you

Your data is yours. Now, and forever.
Play2Worldcoin has never sold user data. Neither will the Play2World Foundation. The Play2World Foundation will create a platform through which PLAY2WORLD holders can choose to monetize their data.
Through this platform, a PLAY2WORLD holder could, for example, choose to engage a health insurer who will pay for access to their Play data. A PLAY2WORLD holder could contribute their data to an aggregate data pool, resulting in features such as global activity indices. Insurers, doctors, academic institutions, and investors would pay for this data – and you, the creator and owner of that data, would receive the reward. The DAO would collect a fee for running the service.
We believe in autonomy. This is a point of principle, and a part of our mission. We believe that each person should own the data they create; and, by owning it, increase their own reward for moving.

PLAY2WORLD Fundamentals

  • TGE will occur in Q3 2022. From announcement April 2022 to TGE, all eligible users of Play2Worldcoin will be able to opt-in with their Play2Worldcoin balances to participate in the TGE at a rate of 1:1, without burning participating Play2Worldcoins, giving an effective rate of 1 point.
  • After TGE, the only way to mint new PLAY2WORLD is by playing, verified by a Play Validator.
  • After TGE, only a user's first 5,000 points will mint PLAY2WORLDcoin; anything above will create Play2Worldcoins. This minting limit is liable to change with tiered minting, explained below.

  • PLAY2WORLD is being built on the NEAR blockchain.

  • At TGE, PLAY2WORLD will be available as an erc-20 Ethereum) token and nep-141 NEAR token.

  • To encourage Play in perpetuity, the Foundation wil introduce an inactivity fee; the DAO will charge users a fee if their activity consistently falls below a given threshold. Not only does this align with our mission of promoting consistent habits, it will also contribute to the exponentially decreasing rate of inflation (the exact details of the fee will be left to PLAY2WORLD holders, who govern the DAO.)

Tokenomics Introduction

The revenue generated by the activities listed below will at first go to the Play2World Foundation, which will drive this value back into PLAY2WORLD. Thereafter, it will go to the Play2World DAO, which will be governed by vePLAY2WORLD (locked PLAY2WORLD for governance).

  • NFTs. 1 Third party NFT providers will have to pay PLAY2WORLD to sell NFTs to users. 2 Users will be able to purchase and upgrade NFTs with PLAY2WORLD, or else by staking considerable amounts of PLAY2WORLD.
  • DeFi functions. The Play2World Wallet will generate fees by enabling a suite of DeFi functions: buying and selling crypto, the exchange of PLAY2WORLD into other crypto, liquidity provision, staking, and more. With the volume of users we have, the accumulated fees will be significant.
  • Access to the audience. Projects who wish to market their tokens or NFTs to the Play2World community will have to pay a fee in PLAY2WORLD, and/or give free/heavily-discounted products to our users.
  • Advertising. With 76M users and counting, the apps Play2Worldcoin and Play2World Wallet App) offer valuable advertising space. Any advertiser must pay in PLAY2WORLD to access this customer base.
  • Alternative Play Validators will have to stake a considerable amount of PLAY2WORLD to operate as a Play Validator (for which they will receive a % fee as per the current Play2WorldCo Ltd validator).
  • Data analytics. The Play2World DAO will give individuals ownership of their data, and offer them the opportunity to monetize that data. As such, any institution, investor, healthcare provider or insurer can buy an individual’s PLAY2WORLD to gain access to data analytics.
  • Premium subscription services. The premium services, such as those already on the Play2Worldcoin App, will include many benefits, such as access to exclusive markets, NFTs, social channels, and more. Users must pay in PLAY2WORLD to access the premium subscription.

The following tokeomics phases indicate the core functionality and utility of PLAY2WORLD. To achieve this, the Foundation will be providing a Play2World Wallet App.


From TGE onwards, we anticipate that PLAY2WORLD will be available on the world's most popular CEXs and DEXs. The first phase of utility for PLAY2WORLD will include:

  • Staking. Earn an attractive % return on PLAY2WORLD.
  • Tiered minting limits. Users who stake/accumulate more PLAY2WORLD will have higher minting limits.
  • Rewards. On-chain and off-chain rewards will be given to holders depending on the amount of PLAY2WORLD they hold/stake.
  • Access to private Telegram and Discord channels in the Play2World community.

  • Dynamic NFTs. More evolved NFTs will return greater rewards and utility, such as a staking multiplier, higher daily minting limits, and on/off-chain rewards. NFTs will need to be fed PLAY2WORLD to evolve and/or prevent decay (represents an inactivity fee).

Tokenomics: phase three 2023/24

  • Crypto marketplace curated offers.

  • Play2World DAO. The Foundation will gradually hand over governance to the Play2World DAO. The revenue generated by the activities listed on the following topics, will go to the Play2World DAO, which will be governed by vePLAY2WORLD (locked PLAY2WORLD for governance).

Tokenomics: phase four 2025 onwards
